Instructions:

The goal of the game is to reach any square on the edge opposite your starting position. Each player receives an equal number of walls (listed by the starting edge) that can be placed to block opponents. Quoridor is a turn-based game. Each turn you may either move or place a wall. You may only move one space, unless you jump an opponent's piece. You may not move through walls. Walls cannot be placed inside eachother or hanging off the edge of the board. No wall can be placed which would block any player from reaching his/her goal.
